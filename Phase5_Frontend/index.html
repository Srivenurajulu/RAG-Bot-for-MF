Mutual Fund Assistant

The Mutual Fund Assistant is available to address factual inquiries concerning ICICI Prudential AMC mutual fund schemes. Specifically, we can provide information related to the expense ratio, exit load, SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) details, ELSS (Equity Linked Savings Scheme) lock-in periods, riskometer assessments, benchmark indices used, and access to relevant statements. Please direct any specific questions related to these areas to the Mutual Fund Assistant for comprehensive assistance.
