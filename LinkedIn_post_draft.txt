I built a facts-only FAQ assistant for ICICI Prudential mutual fund schemes — no investment advice, just answers from official sources.

What it does:
• Answers questions on expense ratio, exit load, minimum SIP, ELSS lock-in, riskometer, benchmark, NAV, and how to download statements
• Refuses advice-style questions and points users to investor education resources
• Rejects queries containing personal information (PAN, Aadhaar, etc.)
• Keeps NAV up to date via a daily scheduler pulling from MFapi.in

Under the hood:
• RAG pipeline: scraped AMC and distributor pages → chunked, embedded with Gemini, stored in ChromaDB
• Hybrid retrieval (vector + keyword, RRF, re-rank) for better relevance
• Fast path: structured data (funds.json) for one-fund factual questions
• Backend: FastAPI; frontend: minimal chat UI with disclaimer and resources page

Scope: 10 schemes (Large Cap, MidCap, ELSS Tax Saver, Multi Asset, Smallcap, Balanced Advantage, Energy Opportunities, Multicap, US Bluechip Equity, NASDAQ 100 Index).

A focused use case for RAG + LLM in a regulated space: factual answers with citations, clear boundaries, and no PII.
